HEY THERE! WELCOME TO OUR SITE

The objective of our work is to increase transparency and accountability of people on media platforms and to prevent any fake profiles being created. This can help in achieving a safe environment, on especially social media platforms and keep our generation safe and sound.

Project Features

Now lets hover over the functonalities.

Gender Detection

It will predict either male or female with utmost accuracy.

Age Detection

It will predict the age of the person and the predicted age may be in one of the following ranges- (0 – 2), (4 – 6), (8 – 12), (15 – 20), (25 – 32), (38 – 43), (48 – 53), (60 – 100)

Deep Learning

Here, Deep Learning is used to accurately identify the gender and age of a person from a single image of a face.

Dataset

The dataset used here is the Adience dataset.This dataset serves as a benchmark for face photos and is inclusive of various real-world imaging conditions like noise, lighting, pose, and appearance.
